TCC members At UB North Campus

TCC at UB North Campus

"The Messiah will suffer and rise from the dead on the third day, and repentance for the forgiveness of sins will be preached in His name to all nations, beginning at Jerusalem." - Luke 24:46-47

The University at Buffalo (UB) is the largest campus in the State University of New York system and New York's leading University.

Now UB is becoming the home of numerous international and American students. India, Pakistan, China, Singapore, Burma, Thailand, Indonesia, Japan, Nigeria, Malaysia, Kenya, and Korea are some of the countries that are in this region. In the mission field, this means these countries have not been completely reached by the Gospel.

Students can be the most efficient and effective missionary vessels for reaching out the lost including students from many different countries. With this vision, on October 10th, 2010 Tabernacle Campus Church was established by devout South Korean minister couple Rev. Hyun and Jinhee Namkung, sponsored by Southern Baptist Convention (SBC) and Korean churches in WNY to plant multi-cultural ministry. TCC values the importance of a spiritual learning community where students are challenged and supported by devout mentors and friends who are Christians. Come and join us, the world is in your hands!

TCC members At UB South Campus

TCC at UB South Campus

"Go rather to the lost sheep of Israel. As you go, preach this message; 'The kingdom of heaven is near.'" - Matthew 10: 6–7

Tabernacle Campus Church at UB South is a Evangelical Christian ministry established solely for the UB South campus. We believe that this establishment has a significant calling for maximum spiritual impact not only for the South campus itself, but also for the surrounding community.

Bordering this campus in the University district of Buffalo/Amherst houses a major population of UB Students. This community also suffers with a high crime rate which is strong evidence that the Love of God is void in this area.

We believe that TCC at UB South is establishing an anointed Godly presence in the South campus community. Our prayer time is directly impacting the students' spiritual life right now. You are always welcome to join us!

TCC members At ECC

TCC at ECC North

"The Messiah will suffer and rise from the dead on the third day, and repentance for the forgiveness of sins will be preached in His name to all nations, beginning at Jerusalem." - Luke 24:46-47

Erie Community College (ECC), with enrollment of more than 13,000 students, is the second largest college in Western New York. ECC offers more than 100 associate degrees, certificates, joint and online programs.

ECC does not have a strong Christian based program on campus, limiting the spreading of the Gospel massage to its students. Tabernacle Campus Church’s (TCC) heart is burdened to act upon Jesus’ commands. The love of our Christ Jesus taught us to go and make disciplines to all nations. Being His disciples, we are willing and ready to take up His cross and follow Him by reaching out to other college campuses.

TCC values the spirituality importance in students’ life. This opportunity is now in place so that the name of our Lord Jesus Christ shall be preached to more people at Erie Community College. Uniting to this calling, a group of devoted TCC members and mentors have bonded together to serve Erie Community College. We are committed to serving and mentoring ECC students. We provide a rich and warm environment for learning and living a Christ-centered life with our multi-cultural worship, faithful prayers, and spiritually nurturing ambiance.

TCC members At Medaille College

TCC at Medaille College

"Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you." - Matthew 28:19-20

Medaille College is an NCAA Division III institution with nearly 1,400 undergraduate students and about 200 graduate and doctoral students enrolled. It is conveniently located at the intersection of Route 198 and Parkside Avenue; mere footsteps away from Delaware Park in Buffalo, NY.

Being obedient to Jesus’ command, Tabernacle Campus Church (TCC) is breaking ground and establishing a presence on campus in Western New York. Medaille College has a limited number of students (especially those who dorm) and the Gospel message of Jesus Christ is absent in the programs on campus there. TCC values the importance of a spiritual learning community where students are challenged and supported by devout mentors and friends who are Christians. We are committed to praying for, learning about, and mentoring the Medaille students.

Students can be the most efficient and effective missionary vessels for reaching out to the lost including students from many different countries. Come join us every Saturday for multi-cultural worship, faithful prayer, and relevant leadership lessons from the Bible to enhance your daily life. TCC promises to equip you as an effective follower of Jesus Christ to help lead and serve the Medaille community. You can't miss it!
